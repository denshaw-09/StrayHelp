About Us

StrayHelp is a community-driven platform dedicated to rescuing and helping stray animals. Our mission is to connect volunteers, NGOs, and animal lovers to provide immediate support for animals in need. Whether it's reporting a stray in distress, uploading a rescue post, or collaborating with local shelters, StrayHelp empowers people to make a difference.
